BAPE has teamed up with Alpha Industries to present an MA-1 and N3-B capsule collection that utilizes the Japanese brand’s iconic motifs. First showcased on display at BAPE’s 25th-anniversary “BAPE XXV” exhibition in 2018, the two jackets are offered in up the American clothing brand’s iconic Replica Gray, further doused with the former’s iconic camouflage print and more.

Sherpa Fleece

Patagonia has delivered its latest series of sustainable garments for the season, releasing an “ultrawarm contemporary jacket” that boasts recyclable properties from the inside out.
